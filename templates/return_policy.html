Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2024

Your satisfaction is our priority. Due to the perishable nature of our products, we have a specific return policy.

Returns

We accept returns under the following conditions:

  • You received a damaged or spoiled product.
  • You received the wrong product.

Please contact our customer service within 24 hours of receiving your order to initiate a return. We will require a photo of the product as proof.

Refunds

Once your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

For "Cash on Delivery" orders, the refund will be processed to your bank account or provided as store credit.

Non-Returnable Items

We cannot accept returns for products due to a change of mind, as they are perishable goods. Please review your order carefully before confirming.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Returns and Refunds Policy, please contact us at agrimarketing@gmail.com.
