Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Our products are sourced directly from farmers upon order confirmation. This minimizes the time from farm to table, ensuring you receive the freshest produce possible.

Click on the "Register" button on the top right. On the registration page, select the "Sell products" option from the dropdown menu and complete the form. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you.

We accept various payment methods including UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Cash on Delivery (COD) in eligible areas.

Orders can be canceled within 2 hours of placement, as we process them quickly to ensure freshness. Please visit your "My Orders" page to cancel. For more details, see our Return Policy.
